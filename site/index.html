Exact user

We are building for the morning care lead, not for a generic shift worker.

The first buyer is a small home-care agency or assisted-living team. The daily hero is the person who starts the morning shift, checks what happened overnight, briefs caregivers, and calls family before the day gets busy.

Night caregiver Leaves short notes during the shift
Morning care lead Turns remembered notes into a safe handoff
Supervisor Prioritizes, corrects, and removes stale memory

Why Cognee is here

Cognee is the memory layer, not a button the caregiver has to learn.

A normal LLM can summarize whatever you paste into the chat. This product needs memory that survives refreshes, sessions, workers, and review cycles. Cognee owns that durable case context.

Caregiver saves a note remember
Morning lead asks for handoff recall
Supervisor marks a source important cognify
Team removes stale context forget
Why this is needed

This is not another chat box for care notes.

Generic LLM workflow

Someone has to paste yesterday's context again. If they forget, the answer starts from zero.

Handoff Memory workflow

The case remembers prior notes, recalls the right sources, improves from review, and forgets stale information.

What the business gets

Faster morning briefings, fewer repeated questions, clearer source history, and safer cleanup of outdated notes.
